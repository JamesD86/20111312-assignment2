Metallica - S&M

It's very difficult to choose a favorite album, but there is one album that I have listened to constantly in my life and that is Metallica featuring The San Francisco Symphony Orchestra - S&M. Released when I was only thirteen years old, S&M combines the greatest heavy metal bands of all time with one of the great composuers of all time. Oh, and he also brought a full orchestra with him.

The most surprising thing about the performance is how well the two very different kinds of music compliment each other. I originally owned a copy of this performance on a CD. Later, I bought a DVD recording of it. Now, it is in my top streamed music on Spotify constantly. There are few things in life that can still provide entertainment after such a long time.
